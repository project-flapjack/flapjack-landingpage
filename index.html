For the devs: The recruiting fee is split up three ways

10% - Developer pool

Developers who get placed and are in our pool get an additional 10% of the pool, divided by number of devs in the pool. This is insurance for in between contracts.

5% - Referral

Developers who refer other developers get this 5% of all of the fee to themselves. We are building a community of self promoting developers this way.
